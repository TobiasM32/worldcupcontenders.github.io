    • Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese soccer player who is one of the greatest soccer players of all time. He has played in 22 total world cup games. Though he never won a world cup series, he was the best player on his team and he has broken multiple professional football records.

    Early Life & Background

    Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5th, 1985 in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He was the youngest of four children in a working-class family. He began playing football at a young age with Andorinha at about 8 years old, then Nacional when he was 10.

    Youth Career

    Ronaldo ended up playing for Lisbon at 12 years old. 3 years later, he was diagnosed with a racing heart condition and had surgery on it. He thankfully recovered quickly and returned to playing for his academy.

    First Professional Debut

    He made his first-team debut on August 14, 2002 vs Inter Milan at 17 years of age. He set the record of the shortest amount of time to go from an academy to a professional team in history.Real Betis at 15 years, 9 months, 16 days of age. This makes him the youngest player in club history. He also broke several other records early in his career.

    Current Status

    Though he is not in his prime anymore, he will likely participate in this upcoming world cup. Something notable about Ronaldo is his great physical shape despite being over 40 years old. Additionally, he is still beating scoring records today, as he scored over 900 official career goals.
