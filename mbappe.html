    • Kylian Mbappe is a French soccer player who participated in two world cups (2018 and 2022 worl cups). In 2028, he became the youngest French player to ever score in a world cup at 19 years old. He and his team additionally ended up winning the world cup that year.

    Early Life & Background

    Kylian Mbappe, Born in December 20th, 1998, was born in Paris, and was raised in Bondy. Growing up in a household centered on sports, Mbappé was immersed in football at an early age.He joined AS Bondy at 6 years old, and his dad was the coach.

    Youth Career

    His Talent got him a spot on France's national football academy, Clairefontaine. His performance there caught the eyes of multiple professional clubs. He later ended up signing with AS Monaco at 14, Which was the start of his journey to becoming a professional footballer.

    First Professional Debut

    He made his first debut in December of 2015. In the 2016-17 season, he helped Monaco win Ligue 1 and making a deep Champions League run. His success resulted in him moving to Paris-Saint-Germain (PSG) in 2017, where he became the most expensive teenager, costing 180 million euros.

    Current Status

    Now, Mpappe is still playing for PSG, and Will be playing in his third world cup after winning his first and making it to the final on his second. He became the youngest player to score a goal in the world cup at 19 years old. He is known for his explosive pace, elite dribbling, and clinical finishing (scoring).
