    • Lionel Messi is an professional soccer player who plays for Argentina in the world cup. He won the previous world cup series, marking his first world cup victory ever. He also won multiple champions leagues and is considered to be the best player of all time.

    Early Life & Background

    Lionel Messi was born in Rosario, Argentina on June 24th, 1987. At 5 years old, he played for Grandoli, where his father would be the coach. At 10 years of age, he was diagnosed with a growth hormone deficiency, which was expensive for his family to treat. Despite this, he continued on his football journey.

    Youth Career

    He joined FC Barcelona's youth academy at 13. They offered to pay for his treatement once he signed for their youth academy, relieving a lot of stress from his family. He then trained at La Masia, FCB's famed academy, where he quickly rose through the ranks thanks to his dribbling, vision, and playmaking.

    First Professional Debut

    He made his first-team debut on October 16, 2004 in a La Liga Match vs. Espanyol. He instantly made a significant impact on the pitch, and was a huge standout. At 17, he became the youngest player to score a goal in La Liga history.

    Current Status

    His appearence in this upcoming world cup series is questionable, as he will be 38 years old when the series begins. In his Prime, however, he won 10 La Liga titles, 4 Champions League titles, 7 Copa Del Rey trophies, and a world cup trophy. He is still widely considered as "the GOAT" to this day. Now, however, he is playing for a lesser professional team, called "Inter Miami".
