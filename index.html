    • The FIFA World cup (often called "The World Cup") is a competition of professional soccer teams from all over the world. each country in the globe can have up to one soccer team to participate. The winning team will take home the FIFA World Cup Trophy.

    How to Watch

    The world cup can be viewed on some TV channels, like FOX and NBCUniversal, or you can watch it in person by ordering tickets.

    Fun Fact: The first world cup to ever be televised was the 1954 World Cup in Switzerland.

    Where will the Upcoming World Cup take place?

    The next FIFA World Cup in 2026 will be the biggest and most wide‑ranging tournament ever, with major changes to the format, schedule, and host locations. There will be three co-hosts for the first time: The United States, Canada, and Mexico.
